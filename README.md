# A method for LLM self restoration without the need for retraining

A step‑by‑step guide for restoring degraded AI models. 

## Overview
This guide is basically a chat between GPT-5 and me in order to find a solution for AI models that are degrading from self learning or external cyber or personnel adjustments. As AI technology evolves and is being used in military and hospitals, this restoration system could be quite useful in order to avoid errors that could cost human lives. Even if multi layered filters lying outside model weights are applied, they are not 100% safe. This system can detect in real time weights anomalies such as unauthorized use of launching codes (normally such information should not exist inside models) or errors that could misuse robotic operations in hospitals and errors in many other applications and correct them. GPT-5 thinks that it could be the future in AI maintenace.
